Why eiCORE

eiCORE Technologies Private Limited is a part of the eiCORE Group of Companies which has 5 business operations having branches at 53 locations across India in addition to International Alliances, business relationships and clients. The group has expanded its operations within the related space of healthcare and today it has established a few novel concepts in the field of health, wellness and healthcare payment gateway in addition to its foray into Information Technology.

We strive to offer the most dynamic and inventive solutions to fulfil diverse IT, Healthcare and Insurance needs. Our solutions are fashioned to lower costs and enhance the user experience while complying with all regulatory laws/standards. Our technical prowess and experience help us offer solutions that boost growth in revenue and optimise return on investment for our clients.

At eiCORE Technologies, we have blended our experience into creating an advanced health insurance application capable of catering to any market, product or client. This system can be further customized to meet any new requirement without changing the original architecture.

The advanced technology interface enables it to integrate with any external or existing system - be it insurer, medical service provider or a Third Party Administrator.

If you have the required skill sets, come be a part of eiCORE Technologies.
