Tech Insurance Solutions Support The Tranformation of your Business

eiCORE is a pure play technology company that provides customized technology solutions to Life, General and Health Insurance Companies, Insurance Brokers, Insurance Aggregators, Third Party Administrators, Health Management Organizations (HMO), Insurance Surveyors and Health Service Aggregators. Our range of products and services suite Property & Casualty, Life and Health Insurance businesses across markets globally.

We at eiCORE, have been constantly upgrading technology platform to continuously meet and exceed the industry benchmarks and have been striving in the direction of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning based product suites to deliver higher process and cost efficiencies.

eiCORE’s “HEALTH BUZZ ECO LINK” is another game changing initiative that is going to change the delivery method of health insurance benefits. “HEALTH BUZZ ECO LINK” with its effortless integration capabilities can connect the health claims systems with providers for a seamless and paperless voyage of health insurance claims.

eiCORE’s asset lite model is the Best solution for smaller businesses in the emerging markets. The user friendly and unique “configurability” feature of the solution ensures modest post sale cost.

We have successfully implemented systems in record time, that can be considered fastest in the insurance industry. Our cost effective SAS model for emerging markets has been put to use in less than 30 days.

Our solutions for the full insurance lifecycle empower your capabilities, productivity and agility.

Artificial Intelligence and Underwriting

With more information available from both traditional and non-traditional sources – motor vehicle records, prescription histories, credit data, etc. – underwriters can now access lengthy histories and view a more dynamic, complete picture of applicants.

Equipping underwriters with tools to access and analyze this additional information is enabling the development of accelerated solutions in which applicants can be underwritten dynamically, based on their behaviors over time, via a more frictionless, consumer-friendly process.
